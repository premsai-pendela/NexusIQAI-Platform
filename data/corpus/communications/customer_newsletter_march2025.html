NexusIQ Customer Newsletter — March 2025

A friendlier returns policy is here

On March 1 we launched version 3 of our Returns and Refunds Policy. Gold and Platinum members now get a 35-day return window on electronics, up from 30 days. Holiday shoppers are covered too: anything purchased between November 1 and December 24 can be returned through January 31. And if you choose store credit, your refund now lands within 24 hours of approval.

Faster refunds, measured honestly

In January our refund processing slipped to an average of 63 hours against our 48-hour goal during the holiday rush. We cleared the backlog by February 10, added processing capacity, and issued goodwill credits to affected customers. March-to-date average: 41 hours.

Sizing help for denim

Fit is the top reason clothing comes back to us. Our updated denim sizing guide — refreshed with measurements from October 2024 onward — is now linked on every jeans product page. Exchanges remain free within the 60-day clothing window.

Coffee lovers, restock is on the way

Coffee has been flying off the shelves (a 90% sell-through this cycle). A 400-unit restock arrives at the North warehouse this week, so subscriptions ship on schedule.

Questions about an order or a return? Reply to this email or visit the support portal. Gold and Platinum members can skip the receipt — we can look up any purchase on your account.
