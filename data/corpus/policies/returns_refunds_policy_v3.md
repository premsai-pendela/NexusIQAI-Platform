# Returns and Refunds Policy — Version 3

Effective March 1, 2025. This version supersedes the Returns and Refunds
Policy effective January 1, 2024. Where the two documents disagree, this
version controls.

## 1. What changed in v3

- The Electronics standard return window is extended from 30 days to
  35 days for Gold and Platinum loyalty members. Bronze and Silver members
  keep the 30-day standard window.
- A holiday extension now applies: purchases made between November 1 and
  December 24 may be returned until January 31 of the following year,
  regardless of category standard window.
- The restocking fee for Electronics preference-change returns is reduced
  from 15% to 10%.
- Store-credit refunds now process within 24 hours of approval, ahead of
  the 48-hour original-payment target.

## 2. Return windows by category (v3)

| Category | Standard Window | Gold/Platinum Window | Defective/DOA Window |
|---|---|---|---|
| Electronics | 30 days | 35 days | 90 days (manufacturer warranty) |
| Home Goods | 45 days | 45 days | 60 days |
| Sports & Fitness | 45 days | 45 days | 60 days |
| Clothing & Apparel | 60 days | 60 days | 60 days |
| Food & Consumables | 14 days (unopened) | 14 days | 30 days (sealed defect) |

Windows begin at confirmed delivery per carrier tracking, not at purchase.

## 3. Unchanged provisions

Eligibility conditions, ineligible item classes (opened food, software
licenses, digital downloads, custom merchandise, Final Sale items), and the
fraud-review triggers (more than three returns in 90 days, or return value
above 60% of annual purchase spend) carry over from the 2024 policy without
modification.

## 4. Processing targets

Refunds to original payment method: initiate within 48 hours of return
receipt. Store credit: within 24 hours of approval. Escalations follow the
Customer Escalation Policy; returns pending more than 10 business days
auto-escalate to a Tier 2 queue.
